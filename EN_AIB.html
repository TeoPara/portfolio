Imageboard

Overview video:

This is a multiplayer website for sharing images.
It's pretty simplistic; there's an upload form where you can select a file and write a comment, and below there's a catalog of all other uploads.

Whatever you upload is saved server-side into a folder, and whenever someone connects, the posts are sent over to be loaded.
If someone else creates a new post while you're watching the catalog, your tab is refreshed.
The date and time of the uploads' creation is also conveyed.

At the top-left there's a checkbox for 'appreciation mode'.
This will expand images to full size & darken the backdrop on mouse-over.

The overview video demonstrates all of the features.
